Our Vision & Mission

Vision

Becoming a reliable and ethical leading platform in providing AI-based weather and analytics data to increase sustainable agricultural productivity in Indonesia and surrounding areas.

Mission

  • Provides High Precision Data: Developing and operating the Agromett weather station networks capable of collecting, analyzing, and predicting real-time agricultural micro-time conditions and hiperlokal with tested accuracy.
  • Encouraging the Intelligent Decision Making: Converting raw data into actionable agronomic recommendations (for example, an optimal irrigation schedule, an early warning of pests/diseases, and best planting time) for farmers through an intuitive interface.
  • Improving Farmers’ Efficiency and Productivity: Helping farmers in saving resources (water, fertilizers, pesticides) and minimizing the risk of crop failure due to extreme weather, thus increasing their harvest and profitability.
