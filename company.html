About Us

AgroMETT is an innovative, low-cost, AI-assisted smart weather intelligence system specifically designed to help farmers monitor local environmental conditions in real time and make better agricultural decisions.

Agromett: Multi-Purpose Weather Station Addressing Agricultural Challenges in Indonesia

We have implemented our tools in Sukoharjo and Banyuwangi. Farmers get data results from our tools, and they can monitor environmental conditions and can anticipate for their crops. Therefore, our tool helps farmers coordinate their rice fields.
